Abstract

TIPZY introduces a scalable, creator-focused token economy built on the Polygon blockchain, designed to transform how appreciation, rewards and micro-payments flow between creators and their communities.

The traditional creator economy is dominated by centralized platforms that restrict global monetisation, impose high fees, delay payouts and limit ownership. TIPZY solves this by enabling instant, low-cost, borderless tipping between fans and creators while returning long-term value to community participants.

Through simple wallet-compatible tools, governance-ready tokenomics and integrations designed for long-term scalability, TIPZY aims to become the default digital currency for creator appreciation across Web2 and Web3. This abstract provides a high-level summary of the TIPZY ecosystem, with deeper detail presented throughout the whitepaper.

5. Ecosystem & Flow

The TIPZY ecosystem connects three primary participants: creators, fans and integrator platforms. TIPZY aims to be simple enough for non-crypto users while remaining fully decentralised on-chain.

  • Creators: Receive TPZY as tips, redeem for value or reinvest into community rewards.
  • Fans: Use TPZY to tip, unlock perks and participate in community events.
  • Platforms: Integrate TIPZY tipping widgets, bots or plugins into their apps.
